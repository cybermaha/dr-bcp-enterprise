# Business Continuity Policy

## Purpose
To ensure uninterrupted delivery of critical services during disruptions.

## Scope
Applies to all IT systems, applications, and departments.

## Policy Statements
- Critical systems must have defined recovery objectives.
- Regular DR drills must be conducted.
- Data backups must be maintained daily.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- IT Head: Overall accountability
- IT Team: Execution
- Management: Approval and oversight
